Attending the Wedding

If you're planning to head straight to Sparrows, this is what we recommend:

Palm Springs Airport (PSP) is the most charming airport we've ever been to. It's adorable. It's also incredibly convenient and close (a 7 minute drive) to Sparrows Lodge. Unless you plan further explorations before or after the wedding, there is no need to rent a car; a taxi or rideshare will work just fine.

There are direct flights to PSP from DFW, Seattle, JFK, Oakland, Denver, Phoenix, LA, and Vegas.

If you would like to travel with other wedding guests, please let us know and we'll put you in touch.

From other airports, Sparrows Lodge is 142 miles (2.5 hours) from San Diego, 277 miles (4-5 hours) from Vegas, and 125 miles (2.5-5 hours) from LA.

We recommend arriving mid-day to evening on Thursday, November 20th, and departing mid-day or later on Sunday, November 23rd. ^

Attending the Rave and the Wedding

For attending the Rave, a car or carpool is mandatory (we can help arrange). Reasonable flight options are:

  • Fly into and out of Palm Springs (PSP).
  • Fly into and out of Vegas (LAS), join us for the convoy down, and drive back after the wedding (make sure to allow enough time).
  • Fly into Vegas, join us for the convoy, and fly out of Palm Springs.

Although it may be possible to fly in early on Wednesday, November 19th and make the Rave before dark, a more relaxed option would be to fly in Tuesday the 18th and spend a night at your arrival city. Fly out mid-day or later on Sunday the 23rd. ^
