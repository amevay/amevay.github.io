Flights This is the week before Thanksgiving and we understand many of you are eager to book flights. The most important choice to make when planning your trip is whether you’re coming to our Desert Rave. If you’re planning to head straight to Sparrows and not participate in the rave, this is what we recommend: Palm Springs Airport, PSP, is the most charming airport we’ve ever been to. It’s adorable. It’s also incredibly convenient and close (a 7 minute drive) to Sparrows Lodge. There are direct flights to PSP from DFW, Seattle, JFK, Oakland, Denver, Phoenix, LA, and Vegas. If you would like to travel with other wedding guests, please let us know and we’ll put you in touch.
