What will the weather be like?

At the end of November, Palm Springs Weather is typically pleasant, with mild daytime temperatures and cooler nights. Daytime temperatures usually range from 70-80°F. Evenings can drop to around 50-60°F. Expect clear skies and no rain.

Are children welcome?

Children listed on your invitation are welcome. Although Sparrows is normally 21+, because we have reserved the whole property, we don't need to abide by this restriction. ^

Do I need to rent a car?

If you are attending only the wedding, and flying into Palm Springs, you can skip the car and use a taxi or rideshare. If you are attending the rave, you will need a car or carpool plan. ^
