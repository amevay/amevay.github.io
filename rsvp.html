How to Attend our Wedding

We are delighted you are considering joining us.

  1. Decide whether you would like to attend the Desert Rave and the wedding (November 19-23), or only the wedding (November 20-23).
  2. Book your stay at Sparrows Lodge using the link on our Lodging page (or your other accommodation).
  3. RSVP via email to wedding at mevay dot org. Please include the following infomation:
    • whether you plan to attend
    • number of people attending
    • whether you will stay at Sparrows Lodge
    • whether you plan to have a car
    • if you plan to attend the Desert Rave
  4. Book your flights, rental car, and/or other travel arrangements. See our Itineraries page for more information.
  5. Check the dress code and other information on our FAQ page.
  6. Of course, please also let us know if you will be unable to attend.
